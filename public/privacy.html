Privacy Policy

Introduction

At ChatCompletions, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our API services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • API usage data and metrics
  • Authentication credentials
  • Request and response content for caching purposes
  • Error logs and debugging information

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our API services
  • Monitor and maintain service performance
  • Optimize caching and response times
  • Generate usage analytics and billing information

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Cached responses are stored in Cloudflare R2 with appropriate access controls and lifecycle policies.

Data Retention

Cached responses are retained according to our lifecycle policies. Usage logs are kept for up to 30 days. You can request deletion of your data at any time through our API endpoints.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your usage history
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through our website or API notifications.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@chatcompletions.com.
